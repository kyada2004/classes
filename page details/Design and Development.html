Desing and development

    (1) Software Design and Development:

  • Software Designers/Architects: Plan and create the overall structure and architecture of software systems.
  • Software Developers/Engineers: Write code and develop software applications based on design specifications.
  • UI/UX Designers: Focus on the user interface and user experience aspects of software, ensuring a user-friendly design.

    • (2) Product Design and Development:

  • Product Designers: Create the aesthetic and functional aspects of physical products.
  • Product Developers/Engineers: Work on turning product designs into tangible, manufacturable items.

    • (3) Web Design and Development:

  • Web Designers: Focus on the visual aspects of websites, including layout and aesthetics.
  • Web Developers: Build and maintain the technical aspects of websites, including coding and functionality.

    • (4)Industrial Design and Development:

  • Industrial Designers: Design products, equipment, and systems for use in various industries.
  • Mechanical Engineers: Develop the mechanical components and systems of industrial products.

    • (5)Graphic Design and Multimedia Development:

  • Graphic Designers: Create visual content for print or digital media.
  • Multimedia Developers: Work on the integration of various media elements like graphics, audio, and video.

    • (6)Architectural Design and Development:

  • Architects: Plan and design buildings and structures.
  • Architectural Engineers: Focus on the engineering aspects of building design.

    • (7)Game Design and Development:

  • Game Designers: Develop the concept, rules, and overall design of video or board games.
  • Game Developers: Code and create the technical aspects of games.

    • (8) System Design and Development:

  • Systems Architects: Plan the overall structure and integration of complex systems.
  • System Developers/Engineers: Implement and maintain the technical aspects of systems.
