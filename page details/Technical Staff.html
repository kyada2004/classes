Technical staff

    (1) IT (Information Technology) Staff:

  • System Administrators: Manage and maintain an organization's computer systems, networks, and servers.
  • Network Engineers: Design, implement, and maintain computer networks.
  • Help Desk Technicians: Provide technical support and assistance to end-users with IT-related issues.
  • Cybersecurity Specialists: Focus on protecting computer systems and networks from security breaches.

    • (2) Engineering Staff:

  • Mechanical Engineers: Design and oversee the manufacturing of mechanical systems and products.
  • Electrical Engineers: Work on the design and implementation of electrical systems and devices.
  • Civil Engineers: Plan, design, and oversee construction and maintenance of infrastructure projects.
  • Software Engineers: Develop and maintain software applications.

    • (3) Healthcare Technical Staff:

  • Biomedical Technicians: Maintain and repair medical equipment.
  • Radiology Technologists: Operate and maintain imaging equipment.
  • Clinical Laboratory Technicians: Conduct tests on medical specimens.

    • (4) Manufacturing and Production Staff:

  • Quality Control Technicians: Ensure that products meet quality standards.
  • Process Engineers: Optimize manufacturing processes for efficiency and quality.

    • (5) Research and Development (R&D) Staff:

  • Research Scientists: Conduct experiments and research to develop new products or processes.
  • Product Development Engineers: Work on designing and improving products.

    • (6)Telecommunications Staff:

  • Telecom Engineers: Design and 0maintain telecommunications systems and networks.
  • Telecom Technicians: Install and troubleshoot telecom equipment.

    • (7)Aerospace and Defense Staff:

  • Aerospace Engineers: Design and develop aircraft and spacecraft.
  • Defense Systems Analysts: Work on the development and maintenance of defense systems.

    • (8) Environmental and Sustainability Staff:

  • Environmental Engineers: Address environmental issues and design solutions.
  • Sustainability Specialists: Focus on implementing sustainable practices within organizations.
