    1. Administrative Support:

  • Office and admin personnel are responsible for handling various administrative tasks such as answering phones, taking messages, organizing meetings, maintaining filing systems, and preparing documents for distribution.

    • 2. Data Management:

  • They are responsible for managing the company's data, including customer information, sales records, and other relevant details.

    • 3. Financial Management:

  • Office and admin personnel are involved in managing the company's financial transactions, such as processing payments, maintaining accounts, and tracking expenses.

    • 4. Communication Management:

  • They are responsible for maintaining effective communication within the company and between the company and its clients or customers.

    • 5. Operational Support:

  • Office and admin staff members play a crucial role in supporting the operational efficiency of the company. They assist in setting up systems, procedures, and processes that facilitate the company's growth and development.

    • 6. Organizational Support:

  • They help in organizing company events, Coordinating employee activities, and promoting a positive work environment.

    • 7. Health and Safety Management:

  • They ensure compliance with health and safety regulations, Monitor safety protocol
